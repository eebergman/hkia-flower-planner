How to use this site

  1. Click on the region (e.g. Merry Meadows)
  2. Click on the flower patch you want (e.g.Fields West)
    More

    If you are unsure of the name of a flower patch there is a button that will link you to Meeve's maps under the header for each region

  3. Now click on the plot you want to "plant" a flower in to open the Edit Plot modal
  1. Click "Color" and choose from the drop down list.
  2. Click "Flower" and choose from the drop down list.
  3. If the flower is pattered you can also select that info.
Note! Once you hit submit if you hover over the plot it will show you it's data. You can also click on the plot to reopen the modal and see what you input.

What is "Mark Plot Empty for New Plants to Grow?"

This will "disable" that plot so you know that is at plot you have planned to leave empty.

Why can't I select "Flower X/Y/X?"

Each flower patch should only show you flowers you are able to plant in that patch as dictated by the rules of the game.
